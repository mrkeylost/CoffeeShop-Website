Product Details

Because of the more toned-down flavour profiles of a lot of Colombian coffees, they make the perfect coffee for mass consumption. Their balance in sweetness, acidity and body makes for many an espresso blend.Blends for espresso are created so as to produce a balanced cup of coffee. Espresso is a concentrated coffee, and so any imbalance in flavour is magnified. Whilst this can be very enjoyable with a heavily diluted filter coffee, it’s disagreeable in espresso as all the characteristics are exaggerated.
